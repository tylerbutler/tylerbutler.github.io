Celebrating 30 Years of Papua New Guinean Independence

PNG celebrated 30 years of independence on September 16, 2005. To celebrate, I decorated my office at work, blew up some balloons, and served Tim Tams. Pictures are in the PNG Independence 2005 Gallery. A few of my co-workers joined in the celebration by putting up balloons and listeneing to PNG music in my office. Not a bad way to waste time on a slow Friday afternoon.
